Coexistence Strategy (during migration)

MUI and shadcn will coexist in the codebase until all components are migrated. This works because:

  • Tailwind uses utility classes — no conflict with MUI's CSS-in-JS (Emotion)
  • shadcn components are just files in src/components/ui/ — no global style injection
  • MUI ThemeProvider stays until the last MUI component is removed
  • next-themes ThemeProvider wraps MUI ThemeProvider temporarily
