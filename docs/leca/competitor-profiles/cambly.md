## Positioning & Messaging

**Primary value proposition**: Personalized English education through real conversations with native speakers, available 24/7.

**Target audience**: All proficiency levels; professionals seeking career advancement, immigrants, adult learners (21+).

**Positioning angle**: Human connection — real native speakers, not AI. "Real English, real progress."

**Key messaging themes**:
- Native speaker tutors available on-demand
- Personalized to individual learning goals
- Real conversations, not scripted drills

---

## Product & Features

### Core capabilities
- 1-on-1 video lessons with native English speakers
- Small group lessons (cost-effective option)
- Lesson recordings and transcripts for review
- Automated feedback on grammar, pronunciation, vocabulary
- Structured courses + self-study content
- Personalized tutor matching
- Mobile app for on-demand sessions

### Notable differentiators
- Human tutors — real native speakers, not AI
- On-demand availability (24/7 tutor pool)
- Lesson recording feature for self-review
- Strong for professionals and immigrants

### AI / Speaking features
- Minimal AI — platform is human-first
- Automated feedback exists but secondary to human tutor
- No AI conversation partner

### Open Source
No. Fully proprietary marketplace.

## Strengths

- Real human conversation — highest authenticity
- Strong for professional/immigrant use cases
- 24/7 tutor availability
- Lesson recordings valuable for self-review
- Established marketplace with large tutor pool

---

## Weaknesses

- Expensive — human tutors cost significantly more than AI
- Quality varies by tutor
- No AI features — won't scale with AI trends
- Not free — no meaningful free tier
- Dependent on tutor availability/scheduling
- Not open source

---

## Competitive Implications

**Their strength vs. us**: Human authenticity, established tutor marketplace  
**Our opportunity**: AI availability 24/7 at fraction of cost, consistent quality, open-source  
**Gap to exploit**: Cambly is expensive and human-dependent — we offer AI-powered conversation at near-zero cost
