// THE INSIGHT

Most people who need English
daily for work have
never used a learning app.

🔁
Exposure doesn't equal improvement
Showing up to meetings and calls every day reinforces existing habits — including bad ones. Real improvement requires deliberate, corrected practice.
💸
Price isn't the barrier — awareness is
It's not that professionals can't afford Cambly or ELSA. They've never heard of them and assume on-the-job exposure is enough.
🎯
Drills don't transfer to real talk
Phonics drills and grammar apps build knowledge, not fluency. Speaking in real conversational context is what makes English feel automatic.
