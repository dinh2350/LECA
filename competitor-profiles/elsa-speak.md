## Product & Features

### Core capabilities
- AI pronunciation analysis at phoneme level
- Bilingual AI Tutor (learns in your native language)
- AI Roleplay scenarios: workplace, job interviews, dating, meetings, presentations
- Avatar coaches (Kevin for workplace, Interview Coach, Riva for dating, etc.)
- Personalized learning path based on learner level, goals, accent, industry
- Progress dashboards with visual skill tracking
- Vocabulary and grammar integrated into speaking exercises

### Notable differentiators
- Most advanced pronunciation AI in market — phoneme-level feedback
- Industry-specific scenarios (workplace English, interview prep)
- Avatar-based roleplay coaches give scenarios a human feel
- Bilingual tutor lowers barrier for beginners

### AI / Speaking features
- Deep AI voice analysis — identifies exact sounds learner struggles with
- Adaptive feedback personalized to individual voice patterns
- AI roleplay across multiple real-world scenarios
- Instant feedback on every utterance

### Open Source
No. Fully proprietary AI model (trained on English phonemes).

## Strengths

- Best-in-class AI pronunciation feedback (phoneme level)
- 92M downloads — strong traction in non-native speaker markets
- Real-world scenario focus aligns with communication goals
- Bilingual tutor removes intimidation for absolute beginners
- Strong enterprise/B2B positioning

---

## Weaknesses

- English-only (no other target languages)
- Focus is pronunciation correction, not free-form conversation
- Scenarios are pre-scripted — limited open-ended dialogue
- Premium pricing for full features
- Not open source — community can't contribute or extend
- Avatar interfaces feel dated vs. modern LLM-powered conversation

---

## Competitive Implications

**Their strength vs. us**: Pronunciation AI depth, brand recognition, 92M users  
**Our opportunity**: Free-form open conversation, open-source model, community-driven scenarios  
**Gap to exploit**: ELSA is a pronunciation drill tool — it doesn't teach you to hold a real conversation spontaneously
